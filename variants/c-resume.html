Summary

Principal Game Designer with a decade in mobile F2P, specializing in character systems, combat design, and live operations. Lead credits on Marvel Strike Force, Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes, and Disney Sorcerer’s Arena — titles with hundreds of millions of installs and multi-year live runs.

My focus is the intersection of systems and feel: designing the character kit that clicks for the player, the content cadence that sustains engagement, the roster depth that rewards time without punishing newcomers. I work in systems because they’re the only way to build that experience at scale — and I stay close to data because it’s how you prove the design is doing what you think it’s doing.
