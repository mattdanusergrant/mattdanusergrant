Formatting cheatsheet
  • # Your Name, then a line for your title, then a line of contacts split by |
  • ## Section: a heading (Summary, Experience, Education)
  • Plain paragraphs under a heading become summary text
  • ### Company, Role @ Dates: starts an entry
  • > note: an italic line under an entry (a credit, a tagline, a course list)
  • - bullet: a bullet point
