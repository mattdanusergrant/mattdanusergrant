Bannerfall

A 3½-minute tactics duel. Build out from your keep, recruit heroes, and storm theirs.

1. Every tile next to yours shows what it builds and its cost. Tap a glowing tile to raise it.
2. You start with one ⛏ Mine — they grow gold and are rare, so grab any you see · ⚔ Recruit (50/100/250) builds a camp that rolls a hero and keeps summoning it — pricier tiles roll rarer heroes · ♜ Tower (100) defends · ? (25) gambles: usually nothing, sometimes a camp.
3. Heroes march the front. Push their color off the map — take their keep, or lead the banner bar at 0:00.
