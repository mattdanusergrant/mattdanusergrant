Chess Frontier

You command a squad of four — a Pawn, Rook, Bishop & Knight — dropped onto an endless checkerboard swarming with rival squads, all roaming, all fighting each other, all coming for you. Crush 6 rival squads to hold the frontier; you lose if your four are wiped out.

It's turn-based and you command every piece. Plan each of your pieces — tap a piece, tap its target — then hit Go and they all move at once; then every other squad takes its move. Real chess moves (rooks slide lines, knights jump, bishops the diagonals). Red-tinted squares are threatened — end a piece off them and nothing can capture it that turn. No clock; take your time.
