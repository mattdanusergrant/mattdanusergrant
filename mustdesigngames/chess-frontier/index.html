Chess Frontier

You are a single pawn on an endless checkerboard. Hunt weaker wild pieces, earn promotions that rewrite how you move (pawn → knight / bishop / rook → queen), and gather a squad. When you reach the crown, an enemy commander rides out of the frontier — capture the enemy Queen to win.

It's turn-based: you move one piece, then every enemy takes a move, then it's you again — no clock, take your time. Tap a highlighted square (dots move, rings capture); tap your own piece to wait a turn. Red-tinted squares are threatened — end your move off them and nothing can capture you that turn. Command your squad with the buttons at the bottom.

A prototype — one screen, one goal: is turn-based chess-piece hunting fun?
