🌙 Map Maker — Collaborator Edition

Design a level for Moon Druid Guardians.
The tiles here render as flat stand-in colours (green = ground, blue = water, dark green = trees). That's on purpose — the real moonlit Wetland art is licensed, so it's painted in later when your map is baked into the game. Your layout, blocking and markers are what carry over exactly.
  1. Shape the ground. Paint terrain, drop 💧 Pond / 🌿 Isle autotiles, stamp 🌳 trees.
  2. Block the impassable. Use ⛔ Block to mark water, cliffs and tree-lines foes can't cross — they'll route around and funnel through gaps.
  3. Place the markers. 🌳 Home (the Elderbough), 💧 Lightwell & 🌲 Copse (harvest nodes), 🌰 Knoll. These become the real gameplay geometry.
  4. Send it back. Hit ⤓ Save map (JSON) and send the file over — it bakes straight into the game.
